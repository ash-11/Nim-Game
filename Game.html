Nim is a mathematical game of strategy in which two players take turns removing (i.e., nimming) objects from distinct row. On each turn, a player must remove at least one object, and may remove any number of objects provided they all come from the same row.

The goal of the game is to avoid taking the last object.

Press row button corresponding to the row from which you want to remove object. After each move press 'PC move' to make the PC play.
